The Founder

The founder of the Met Gala, Eleanor Lambert, envisioned an extraordinary event that transcended the realms of fashion, art, and culture.

Being an innovator, Lambert established a platform independent from conventions, where creativity and self-expression took center stage.

Lambert's unwavering commitment to curating an unforgettable experience placed the focus squarely on pushing boundaries and showcasing the boundless imagination of designers and celebrities.

Today, the Met Gala, a testament to Lambert's vision, continues to captivate the world, setting trends and leaving an indelible mark on the ever-evolving landscape of the fashion industry.


The Venue

Located in the heart of Putrajaya, Heriot-Watt University Malaysia serves as the esteemed venue for our exclusive Met Gala dinner.

Set against the backdrop of the university's stunning campus, the event will take place in a specially transformed space that captures the essence of glamour and sophistication.

Heriot-Watt University Malaysia provides the perfect blend of modern facilities and architectural elegance, offering a unique atmosphere for an unforgettable Met Gala dining experience.

Join us at Heriot-Watt University Malaysia as we celebrate fashion, art, and culture in an enchanting setting, creating memories that will last a lifetime.


The Theme

Step into the enchanting world of the Regency Era as we transport you to the captivating universe of Bridgerton for our Met Gala dinner at Heriot-Watt University Malaysia.

Our theme pays homage to the opulence, romance, and elegance depicted in the popular Bridgerton show, inviting guests to embrace the fashion and allure of the Regency Era.

Immerse yourself in a night of grandeur and sophistication as we recreate the ambiance of high society balls, scandalous gossip, and the timeless charm of Bridgerton.

Prepare to be swept away by the regal setting, breathtaking decor, and meticulously curated experiences that will transport you to a world where romance and intrigue intertwine, making this Met Gala dinner an affair to remember.

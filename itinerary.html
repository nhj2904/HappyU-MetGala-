6:00 PM - Opening Speech

Gather for an inspiring opening speech that sets the tone for the evening, highlighting the significance of this milestone and the memories that await.

Sit down for a delicious dinner prepared by professional caterers, offering a variety of delectable dishes.

Enjoy a glamorous fashion show, featuring the latest trends and showcasing the incredible style and creativity of your fellow classmates.

Witness the crowning moment as the Prom King and Queen are announced, honoring their contributions to the school community.

Take part in a memorable photography session, capturing beautiful moments with your friends and creating lasting mementos of this special night.

10:00 PM - Last Dance

End the night with an emotional and memorable last dance, creating lasting memories with your friends and classmates.
